Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • 500g Boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 tbsp All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • 1 tbsp Rice flour
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Yogurt
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Fresh curry leaves
  • 2-3 Green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine cornflour, all-purpose flour, and rice flour.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  3. Crack the egg and add it to the mixture. Add yogurt and mix well to form a thick marinade.
  4. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix until all pieces are well coated. Cover and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully drop the marinated chicken pieces into the oil.
  6. Fry until the chicken pieces are golden brown and crispy. Remove the fried chicken pieces using a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. In another pan, heat a little oil. Add fresh curry leaves and slit green chilies and saut√© for a few seconds.
  8. Add the fried chicken pieces to the pan and toss them with the curry leaves and green chilies. Drizzle with lemon juice for extra flavor.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
