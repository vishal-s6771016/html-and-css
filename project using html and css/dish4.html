Parotta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Water (or as needed)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine all-purpose flour, oil, and salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead to form a soft and pliable dough.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls.
  5. Roll out each ball into a thin circle.
  6. Apply a thin layer of oil on the surface of the rolled-out dough.
  7. Fold the dough into a thin strip and then coil it into a round shape.
  8. Gently flatten the coiled dough with your hands or a rolling pin.
  9. Heat a griddle or tawa over medium heat. Add a little oil.
  10. Place the flattened dough on the griddle and cook until golden brown on both sides, adding more oil as needed.
  11. Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls.
  12. Serve hot Parottas with your favorite curry or gravy.
