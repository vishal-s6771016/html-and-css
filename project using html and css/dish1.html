Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 Romaine Lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 Bell Pepper, sliced
  • 1 Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 Carrot, grated
  • 1 Avocado, chopped
  • 1/4 cup Olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup Feta Cheese, crumbled (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and prepare all the vegetables.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, grated carrot, avocado, and olives.
  3. If using, add the crumbled feta cheese on top.
  4. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Prepare a simple dressing by whisking together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss again to coat all the ingredients.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh vegetable salad!
