Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken pieces
  • 2 Onions, finely chopped
  • 3 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • 1 cup Water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  5. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat them with the spices. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the chicken starts to brown.
  7. Add water and bring it to a boil. Lower the heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
